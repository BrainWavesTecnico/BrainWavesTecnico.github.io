Decoding the brain,
one wave at a time.

The BrainWaves research group, led by Joana Cabral, is part of the Evolutionary Systems and Biomedical Engineering Lab (LaSEEB), at the Instituto Superior Técnico (IST), the engineering school of the University of Lisbon. It is integrated in the Institute for Systems and Robotics (ISR-Lisboa), a research unit from the Laboratory for Robotics and Engineering Systems (LARSyS) in the LIFE Thematic Line.

BrainWaves research group investigates brain dynamics, focusing on emergent properties, such as collective oscillations, long-range correlations, resonance and non-stationary behaviour. The group combines neuroimaging analysis and signal processing with dynamical system's theory, computational modelling, and physical experiments to understand the mechanisms behind the observed dynamics and their link with cognition and mental health.
